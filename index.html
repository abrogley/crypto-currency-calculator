Overview

Here are some useful client-side tools for your crypto currency calcuation needs. If you are worried about security, download the code, examine it for any issues, then to make extra sure, run it locally in your browser in private/incognito mode with your Internet connection turned off.

  • Capital gains and losses

Capital gains and losses

Calculates the capital gains generated by transactions using the FIFO accounting method. The input for this calculation is the date of transaction, number of coins involved, and the cost basis of the transaction. See bitcointax.info for more.
